about the writer

starlit_roulette

she/they · EST (UTC-5)

Hi! I’m a longtime Hunter x Hunter fan and fic writer who loves character-driven stories, complicated hearts, and the quiet moments that say everything.

Kurapika has my whole entire heart. The Phantom Troupe fascinate and devastate me in equal measure. I live for recovery arcs, found family, and the kind of hope that doesn’t erase the pain — but helps you carry it.

thanks for being here. i hope my stories stay with you. ✧
